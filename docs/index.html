Models for sustainable inland fisheries

This is a webpage of models developed during the project “Advanced models, citizen science and big data for sustainable food production and ecological services of inland aquatic ecosystems”. The project has received funding from European Regional Development Fund (project No 01.2.2-LMT-K-718-02-0006) under grant agreement with the Research Council of Lithuania (LMTLT).

This page and models presented here have been created by Asta Audzijonyte, Carl Smith, Catarina Silva, Egle Jakubavičiūtė, Dalia Grendaitė, Max Lindmark, Freddie Heather, Steve Midway and Gustav Delius.
